Services

We offer a comprehensive range of services to our patients across our hospital, as well as in the community.

Aged Care

We lead the way in aged care, providing specialised medical care for people over 65 years for issues including dementia, falls, continence problems and rheumatology.

Community Services

We run a range of outpatient and community clinics to help people when they leave hospital as well as programs designed to prevent people from needing hospital care.

Emergency & Intensive Care

Well known for being a major trauma service, we run two very busy emergency departments and have one of the largest Intensive Care Units, offering the best care in the country.

Diagnosis & Investigation

Diagnosing medical issues early is crucial. Whatever your condition, we can diagnose it using a range of technologies and pathologies.

Medical & Surgical

We have the most comprehensive medical services in the state, with expert care covering a wide range of illnesses plus emergency and elective surgery, ensuring our community receives the care they need.

Mental Health

We provide a range of specialised mental health services for people of all ages in our community.

Rehabilitation

We are a statewide provider of rehabilitation services, helping people after accident or illness to regain function, independence and wellbeing.

Specialised Support Services

Your care will be multi-disciplinary to ensure you can return to your daily activities as soon as possible.

Financial Services

We accept all insurance. (VA accepted with prior approval.) For your convenience, we accept Visa, MasterCard, and Discover.
